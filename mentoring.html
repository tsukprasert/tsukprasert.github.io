Mentoring

I have had the privilege of mentoring undergraduate students through the Undergraduate Research Volunteer (URV) and Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) programs. Below are posters from their research projects.

Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) — 2025
Mary-Alice Wieland
Analyzed carbon reduction trends from spatiotemporal datacenter workload data spanning 2021–2024. Presented at Massachusetts Gender-Inclusive Computing Celebration (MAGICC) 2025.
Undergraduate Research Volunteer (URV) — Summer 2025
Himnish Chhabra, Nishil Adina
Built a carbon-aware load balancer that dynamically routes workloads to low-carbon servers.
Undergraduate Research Volunteer (URV) — Winter 2025
Lindsey Blau, Sabrina Nguyen, Sampada Tumuluri
Studied the discrepancy between marginal and average carbon intensity signals.
Undergraduate Research Volunteer (URV) — Summer 2024
Anh Trinh, August Huber, Khang Do, Pranav Ravi Buregoni
Developed machine learning models to forecast carbon intensity in electricity grids.
Undergraduate Research Volunteer (URV) — Winter 2024
Ruth Barasa, Gabriel Laboy, Farhana Rahman, Megan Wong
Investigated temporal trends in electricity grid carbon intensity from 2021 to 2023.
